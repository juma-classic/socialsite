Data Deletion Request

⚠️ Important: This page allows you to request deletion of all your data from Social Sight. This action cannot be undone.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • All connected social media account information
  • Historical analytics and metrics data
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Any cached or stored data related to your accounts

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Through the App

You can disconnect your accounts directly in the Social Sight dashboard:

  1. Go to Settings → Connected Accounts
  2. Click "Disconnect" for each connected account
  3. Your data will be automatically scheduled for deletion

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

To request immediate data deletion, please contact us with the following information:

Email: privacy@socialsight.app

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • The social media accounts you connected (username/handle)
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted
  • Your preferred method of confirmation

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: We stop collecting new data from your accounts
  • Within 48 hours: Your data is removed from our active systems
  • Within 30 days: All backups and cached data are permanently deleted

What Happens After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer see any analytics from your previous connections
  • Your data cannot be recovered
  • You can reconnect your accounts in the future, but historical data will not be available

Retention of Some Information

For legal and security reasons, we may retain some information for a limited time:

  • Usage logs (without personal identifiers) for security purposes
  • Information required by law or regulation
  • Data necessary to resolve disputes or enforce our terms

Questions About Data Deletion

If you have questions about the data deletion process, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@socialsight.app
  • Response Time: We typically respond within 2 business days

This page satisfies the data deletion requirements for Facebook/Instagram app approval and other social media platforms.
